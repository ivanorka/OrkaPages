KPI hierarchy

KPI sustav povezuje prodaju, marketing, delivery, recurring i cash.

KPI-evi nisu reporting dekoracija. Oni su mehanizam za mjesečne odluke: što skalirati, što ugasiti i gdje povećati cijene.

Company

Prihod, EBITDA / EBIT, bruto marža, cash runway, revenue per FTE.

Sales

Weighted pipeline, win rate, sales cycle, forecast accuracy, naplaćeni prihod.

Marketing

MQL, SQL, inbound upiti, cost per lead i case studies.

Delivery

Planirani vs stvarni sati, kašnjenja, scope creep i change request revenue.

Recurring

MRR, ARR, churn, NRR, SLA response i support margin.
